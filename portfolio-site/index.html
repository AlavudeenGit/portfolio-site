About the Artist

A decade spent between the pencil and the pixel.

I'm Rahib, a fine artist and graphic designer working across two disciplines that rarely share a studio: hand-rendered portraiture and digital brand design. My practice began with charcoal and graphite, chasing likeness and light on paper; it grew to include oil, acrylic and watercolor, then expanded again into logo and identity work for small businesses and creative studios.

Every project — whether a commissioned family portrait or a full visual identity — starts the same way: careful looking, then careful making. I believe restraint is a skill, and that the best design gets out of the way of the story it's telling.
